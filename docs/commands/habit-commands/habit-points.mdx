# /habit points 

See the XP (points) you have gained from completing your daily and weekly habits. Use this command to track your progress and celebrate your consistency as you build healthy routines.

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 💬 Usage
```bash
/habit points
```

:::warning
No options or inputs are required—just run the command to check your current habit XP.
:::

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 📌 Example
```bash
/habit points
```
This will:
- Display an embed in your current channel or DM showing your total habit XP (points)
- Give you a summary of the points earned by completing your tracked habits
- Help you stay motivated as you watch your progress grow over time

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 🗃️ Notes
> - XP is earned each time you complete a habit using Elysium’s tracking system
> - The embed may include a breakdown of points by habit type or frequency
> - This command is a great way to gamify your habit journey and celebrate milestones

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 🔍 Related Commands
`/habit list` – View your current habits

`/habit stats` – See detailed stats for a specific habit

`/habit add` – Add a new habit to track

`/habit remove` – Delete a habit you no longer want to track
