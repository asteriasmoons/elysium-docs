# /tbr remove 

Remove a book from your TBR (To Be Read) list. Use this command to clear books you’ve finished, decided not to read, or just want to keep your list tidy.

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 💬 Usage
```bash
/tbr remove <title> <author>
```
- `<title>` – The title of the book you want to remove
- `<author>` – The author of the book

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 📌 Example
```bash
/tbr remove "Legends & Lattes" "Travis Baldree"
```
This will:
- Permanently delete “Legends & Lattes” by Travis Baldree from your TBR list
- Help you keep your reading plans up to date

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 🗃️ Notes
> - Only books you’ve added to your own TBR list can be removed
> - Use this to clear finished, abandoned, or unwanted books
> - Removing a book from your TBR list does not affect anyone else’s list
> - Double-check the title and author for accuracy before removing

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 🔍 Related Commands
`/tbr add` – Add a book to your TBR list

`/tbr list` – View your TBR list or another user’s
