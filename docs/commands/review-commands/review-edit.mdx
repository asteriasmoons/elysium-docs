# /review edit 

Edit an existing book review you submitted. Use this command to update your written review and/or star rating for a book and author—perfect for revising your thoughts, correcting typos, or changing your score after reflection.

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 💬 Usage
```bash
/review edit <book> <author> <review> <rating>
```
- `<book>` – The title of the book you reviewed
- `<author>` – The author of the book
- `<review>` – Your new or updated review text
- `<rating>` – Your new star rating (e.g., 1–5)

:::info
**You may edit your review as many times as you need.**
:::

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 📌 Example
```bash
/review edit "Legends & Lattes" "Travis Baldree" Even more enjoyable on a second read! 5
```
This will:
- Update your review for “Legends & Lattes” by Travis Baldree with your new review text and a 5-star rating
- Replace your previous review and rating for that book/author combination
- Confirm the changes with a message or embed

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 🗃️ Notes
> - You can only edit reviews you’ve written
> - Editing your review overwrites the previous text and rating
> - If you want to delete a review instead, use `/review delete`
> - Star ratings should be whole numbers (1–5)

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 🔍 Related Commands
`/review add` – Add a new review

`/review delete` – Remove your review

`/review book` – View reviews for a book

`/review author` – View reviews by author

`/review list` – Browse all reviews

`/review view` – Shortcut to view book reviews
