# /review add 

Add a new book review to Elysium’s global collection. Use this command to share your thoughts on a book—along with a star rating and your own review text—for the benefit of the whole community.

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 💬 Usage
```bash
/review add <book> <author> <rating> <review>
```
- `<book>` – The title of the book you’re reviewing
- `<author>` – The author of the book
- `<rating>` – Your star rating for the book (e.g., 1–5)
- `<review>` – Your written review (share your thoughts, impressions, and recommendations)

:::info
**Please make sure that youre reviews are honest.**
:::

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 📌 Example
```bash
/review add "Legends & Lattes" "Travis Baldree" 5 Absolutely charming and cozy—loved every minute!
```
This will:
- Add a 5-star review for “Legends & Lattes” by Travis Baldree to Elysium’s global review library
- Share your review and rating for others to read
- Make your review discoverable by book, author, or via the global review list

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 🗃️ Notes
> - Reviews are global—visible to all Elysium users
> - You can only have one review per book per author; editing or deleting is allowed
> - Keep reviews constructive and respectful for community benefit
> - Star ratings typically use integers (1–5); half-stars may not be supported

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 🔍 Related Commands
`/review book` – View reviews for a specific book

`/review author` – See reviews by author

`/review edit` – Update your review and rating

`/review delete` – Remove your review

`/review list` – See all reviews

`/review view` – Shortcut to view reviews for a book
