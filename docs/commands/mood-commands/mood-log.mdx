# /mood log

Log your current mood and activities with an interactive embed. This command opens a guided check-in process: choose your mood and activities from select menus, add an optional note, and click a button to save your entry. It’s a quick, private way to reflect and track your emotional wellbeing over time.

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 💬 Usage
```bash
/mood log
```

:::success
**No options or inputs are required—just run the command to begin your check-in.**
:::

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 📌 Example
```bash
/mood log
```
This will:
- Send you an embed (in channel or DM) with dropdown menus to select your current mood and recent activities
- Provide a button to add an optional note about your mood or day
- Offer a final button to log and save your mood entry
- Automatically store your entry for mood tracking and stats

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 🗃️ Notes
> - Only you can view and access your logged mood entries—entries are private
> - Choose from a variety of moods and activities
> - Mood logs can help you identify trends, triggers, and patterns in your wellbeing
> - Entries can be analyzed later with `/mood stats`

<hr className="md-divider-gradient" />

### 🔍 Related Commands
`/mood stats` – View your mood statistics and trends

`/mood remind set` – Set up reminders for mood logging

`/mood remind status` – Check the status of your mood reminder

`/mood remind off` – Turn off mood reminders
